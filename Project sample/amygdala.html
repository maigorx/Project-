Amygdala – The Drama Queen 😱

This part of your brain is basically the “OMG!” center. It freaks out when there’s danger. That’s great when there’s an actual lion chasing you... but not so chill when it’s just a math test 😩.

🧠 What it does + how to calm it down

If your amygdala is always like “RED ALERT 🚨,” you might feel anxious, jumpy, or panicky — even if nothing’s really wrong. It’s like your brain’s fire alarm is too sensitive.

Solutions that help it chill:

  • 🌬️ Breathe it out: Try 4-7-8 breathing (inhale 4 sec, hold 7, exhale 8)
  • 🎧 Listen to calm music: Lo-fi, nature sounds, or whatever soothes you
  • 🧘‍♀️ Ground yourself: 5-4-3-2-1 trick (name what you see, feel, hear, etc.)
  • 📓 Write it down: Journaling can help you spot when your brain’s overreacting
