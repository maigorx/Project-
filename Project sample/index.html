🧘‍♀️ Self-Care Tips

  • Breathing Exercise: Try the 4-7-8 technique (Inhale 4s, Hold 7s, Exhale 8s)
  • Journal Prompt: "What's something small that brought me peace today?"
  • Grounding Technique: 5-4-3-2-1 method: name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you can hear, etc.

🧠 Brain & Mental Health Connections

Amygdala – The Alarm Bell

It controls fear & fight-or-flight. Overactive? Hello anxiety and panic attacks.

🧠 Mental Health Info

  • Anxiety: When your brain’s like "what if?" on loop. Normal, but exhausting.
  • Depression: A mental fog that makes everything harder. You're not alone.
  • Burnout: When you’ve been running too long without rest. Recharging is healing.
